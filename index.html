Our full vision for the Rail Park is to
create a three-mile greenway with three
sections: the Viaduct, the Cut, and the
Tunnel. It will reach from Northern
Liberties to Fairmount Park, with
pathways and gathering spaces that will
serve pedestrians, bicyclists, neighbors,
and visitors alike.
